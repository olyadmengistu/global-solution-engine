Help & Support

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I post a problem?

Click on the "What problem are you facing today?" input box at the top of your feed, type your problem description, and click "Post Problem". You can also add images or files to provide more context.

How do I offer solutions to problems?

Below each problem post, you'll see an "Offer Solution" button. Click it to provide your solution. The best solutions are voted on by the community and can earn you recognition points.

How do I connect with other users?

Visit the "My Network" page to find and connect with other problem solvers. You can search for users, view their profiles, and send connection requests to build your professional network.

What are the privacy settings?

You can control who sees your profile and activity through the Settings menu. Choose between Public, Connections Only, or Private visibility for your profile and posts.

How do I edit my profile?

Click on your profile picture in the top navigation and select "Edit Profile". From there you can update your personal information, add social links, and manage your privacy settings.
